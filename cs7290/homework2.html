Beer Prices at Major League Baseball Games

What's more American than some good old fashioned beer and baseball? One of my new favorite pasttimes (since turning 21 in July, obviously), has been having a few brews and watching the New York Mets torment me with their inability to win a god damn baseball game. Now, everyone knows that stadiums like to rip you off and charge way too much for my Miller Lite, so I wanted to see how bad my beloved New York Mets were overcharging me. I have luckily discovered that the Giants and Red Sox take advantage of their patrons more than the Mets, and we were cheaper than our cross-town rivals the Stankees (oops, I mean Yankees) until only recently. Maybe the additional revenue from beer sales will allow us to afford a team that can actually win ballgames, and doesn't spend half the season on the IL. Anyways, LFGM and we better make the postseason in 2022 if you're going to continue to price gouge my alcoholism.
